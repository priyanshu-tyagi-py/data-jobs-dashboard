# Global Data Jobs Market Analysis Dashboard.

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes global demand for data jobs, salary trends, and geographic distribution of job opportunities.

## Features:
- Top data job roles by demand
- Global job distribution map
- Salary insights (median & average)
- Interactive filtering by country

## Tools Used:
- Power BI
- DAX

## Insights & Findings:
- The high demand for Data Engineers suggests organizations are prioritizing data infrastructure and scalable pipelines  
- Strong presence of Data Analyst roles indicates continuous need for business intelligence and reporting  
- Geographic concentration in developed regions points to maturity of data ecosystems in those markets  
- Salary disparities across countries highlight the influence of economic factors and market demand on compensation  
- Variation in hourly vs yearly pay reflects diversity in employment types, including contract and full-time roles
